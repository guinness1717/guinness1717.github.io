About

Born in Stockton, CA, Olivia is the youngest of seven. After graduating from high school in 2006, she attended college at the University of California, Santa Cruz until 2010. She spent the majority of her time in California and recently moved to New Orleans in 2014. She lives with her wonderful girlfriend and two annoying cats.

Olivia's interest in technology has always existed, but it came to a head when she began working for a start-up in San Francisco. She was on the company website and said "Who do I talk to about coming up with product features?" Her boss replied "The product manager!" After that, she was determined to understand, build, and create new products and ideas. She's constantly thinking of solutions to problems ranging from dehydration to software fixes for police officers and the public sector.
